Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 grams minced beef
  • 800 grams canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper
  • 475 grams ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 handful fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 225 grams mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50 grams grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. First, preheat your oven to 180°C. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil and add 1 diced onion and 2 minced cloves of garlic, sautéing until the onion is translucent.
  2. Then add 500 grams of minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in 800 grams of canned crushed tomatoes, 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Let this simmer for 20 minutes. In another bowl, combine:
    • 475 grams of ricotta cheese
    • 1 beaten egg
    • a handful of chopped fresh basil
    • and a pinch of salt.
  5. For the lasagna assembly, spread a spoonful of the meat sauce in a baking dish, then layer:
    • 3 lasagna sheets
    • half of the remaining meat sauce
    • another 3 lasagna sheets
    • all the ricotta mixture
    • another 3 lasagna sheets
    • the rest of the meat sauce
    • and finally sprinkle 200 grams of grated mozzarella cheese on top
  6. Cover with aluminium foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  7. After this, remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
