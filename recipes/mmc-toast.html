Marmite, mayonnaise and cheese on toast

Ingredients

  • Marmite
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Bread (toasted)

Directions

  1. First, toast your bread.
  2. Then spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on the toast.
  3. Then spread a thin layer of Marmite on the toast.
  4. Then add a layer of cheese.
  5. Then cut the toast and serve while hot.
